09 — Summary

Key Findings

Key insights from the EDA process on the bodybuilding nutrition product dataset.

⚠️
Serious missing values in 3 columns number_of_flavors, top_flavor_rated, and average_flavor_rating are all missing >54% of data — recommend removal before model training.
🎯
Well-balanced target The ratio is 50.36% / 49.64% (Standard/Premium) — the dataset is not imbalanced, so oversampling is not required.
📊
Heavily skewed reviews Mean 483 vs median 95 — extremely strong right-skewed distribution. Recommend log transform before inputting into the model.
🏋️
Whey Protein accounts for 72.8% of Premium Whey Protein Isolate (85.3%) and Improve Workout Products (74.2%) have the highest Premium rates.
🔗
High correlation between flavors & reviews r = 0.747 between the number of flavors and number of reviews — products with diverse flavors attract more users.
⭐
Very high overall ratings (mean 8.95/10) Most products are well-rated. Low-end outliers (rating < 7.3) only account for 2.86%.
