08 — Summary

Key Findings

Distilled insights from the full EDA pipeline.

⭐
Overwhelmingly Positive Reviews Over 55% of all reviews gave a 5-star rating. The average rating of 4.20 confirms high customer satisfaction across the board.
💬
Customers Care About Fit The top words — "small," "large," "fits," "petite," "waist" — reveal that sizing and fit are the #1 concern in reviews.
👗
Tops Dominate the Catalog The Tops department generates the most reviews by far, making it the most reviewed and arguably most popular category.
📊
Recommendation Rate is High 82.2% of reviewers would recommend the product they purchased, a strong signal of overall brand loyalty and satisfaction.
👩
Core Demographic: 34–52 Years Old The interquartile range of reviewer age is 34–52, with a median of 41. Younger (18–25) shoppers are underrepresented.
🔤
Rich, Descriptive Language With 13,049 unique words across 455K+ tokens, the review corpus is linguistically diverse — a strong base for NLP classification tasks.
